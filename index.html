üåç Sustainable Chemistry in Action

Green chemistry aims to design products and processes that minimize waste and energy use while protecting our planet.

  • Use renewable materials instead of fossil fuels.
  • Minimize waste through efficient reactions.
  • Replace toxic solvents with water or natural alternatives.
  • Conduct reactions at room temperature to save energy.

Example: Creating biodegradable plastics from corn starch instead of petroleum-based materials!
