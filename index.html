🔬 STREAM Connections

Science 🌱

Green chemistry focuses on minimizing waste and using renewable materials. It connects chemistry with biology (biodegradable materials) and environmental science.

Technology 💻

This app uses JavaScript to calculate data, simulate reactions, and make learning interactive—showing how tech helps scientists visualize real-world chemistry.

Engineering ⚙️

Engineers design eco-friendly chemical reactors, solar-powered labs, and efficient processes that reduce emissions and energy use.

Art & Design 🎨

Design plays a key role in sustainability! From app colors inspired by nature to infographics spreading awareness, creativity helps communicate science effectively.

Math 📊

Math helps calculate reaction yields, carbon footprints, and efficiency. Try changing your energy/waste inputs in the calculator and observe the data change!

Eco Tips & Innovations

  • ♻️ Use catalysts to reduce energy consumption in reactions.
  • 🌱 Replace toxic solvents with water or bio-based alternatives.
  • ☀️ Harness solar energy for chemical synthesis.
  • 💧 Design biodegradable materials to reduce pollution.
  • 🌍 Support green chemistry education and awareness.
  • 🚯 Reduce single-use plastics by choosing reusable lab materials.
  • 🌡️ Use digital tools to model reactions before performing experiments.
